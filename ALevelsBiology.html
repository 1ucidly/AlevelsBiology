How do we Percieve Risk?

Generally, people overestiamte the risk of sudden imposed dangers & underestimate the risk if it has an effect in the long term.

Factors affecting risk perception include:

  1. Control over the outcome
  2. How natural is it(flying on a plane is not natural)
  3. How familiar is it
  4. How fair is it(if you win you get 1 dollar, if you lose you die)

Correlations
